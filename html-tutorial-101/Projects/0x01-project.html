Favorite Recipe: Chocolate Chip Cookies

Dish Description

Warm, gooey, and packed with chocolate chips — these cookies are a timeless treat.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 3 cups flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 2 cups chocolate chips

Preparation Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Cream together butter and sugars.
  3. Add eggs and vanilla; mix well.
  4. Stir in flour, baking soda, and salt.
  5. Fold in chocolate chips.
  6. Drop spoonfuls onto a baking sheet and bake for 10-12 minutes.

Cooking Tips

For chewier cookies, underbake slightly and let them finish cooking on the sheet.
